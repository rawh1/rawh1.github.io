Rulia Joberts

Rulia has worked on large-scale residential and mixed-use projects and was a Teaching Assistant for undergraduate and graduate studios at the Harvard Graduate School of Design (GSD).

Rulia holds a Master in Architecture from the Harvard GSD. and a Bachelor of Science in Architecture with a Minor in Urban Design from Washington University in St. Louis, MO. Julia enjoys exploring new neighborhoods, cities, and countries; and enjoys being outdoors.
